Hibernate, Hibernate
O/R Mapping sure is great
Gavin King, and H-Q-L

Mapping Files in Hibernate

Hibernate, Hibernate
Friendly neighborhood Hibernate
Grab the jars, and climb aboard

To it…

You need the Hibernate!
