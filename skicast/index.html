Skicast

Ceres Logic is proud to announce the follow-up to its first Android application, Tidecast, with Skicast.  Skicast is a mobile application that allows users to get the latest information on ski resorts in real-time.  Through a partnership with SnoCountry.com, Skicast is able to provide the following information on ski areas in North America, Europe, and other ski areas in the southern hemisphere:

  • Open Alpine and Nordic trails
  • Trail conditions
  • Night Grooming
  • Trail Maps
  • Web Cam Views
  • Contact Information and Driving Directions
  • Weather Forecasts and Recent Snowfall totals

Skicast Pro Edition also features two different sized home screen widgets so you can get at-a-glance updates for your favorite resorts.  Download it from the Android Market before you hit the slopes!
