Tidecast

Tidecast is an Android mobile application that displays tidal forecasts for nearly 3,000 locations around the United States. It downloads tidal information from NOAA, and calculates the moon phase and sunrise and sunset times. Â Tidecast can determine the closes tidal station to your current location using the GPS feature of your phone.

One of Tidecast’s nicest features is its homescreen widget, which displays the current tidal forecast for either the closest tide station, or the station of your choosing.
